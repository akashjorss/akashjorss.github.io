About

I am a PhD student at LISN (Université Paris-Saclay), supervised by Prof. Nacéra Seghouani, in a CIFRE partnership with Amadeus. My research focuses on novel view synthesis from casual captures, spanning 3D Gaussian Splatting, multiview diffusion models, and neural field theory.

My thesis, Approaches for High-Fidelity Novel View Synthesis: From Casual Captures to Novel Views, addresses frame selection for reconstruction, generative view synthesis for filling in missing views, and theoretical foundations connecting neural rendering to computation.
